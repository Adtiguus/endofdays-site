// questions

Straight answers.

Does it really work with zero internet?

Yes. The AI model and the entire library live on the box. At runtime there is no uplink, no account, no cloud call. It even makes its own WiFi so your phone can reach it anywhere.

Why does "it tells you when it's guessing" matter?

Because a confident wrong answer is the dangerous one. Every answer is grounded in a real manual and shows its source. When the library doesn't cover something, it says so — flagged, in red — instead of bluffing.

Do I need to be technical?

Not for the done-for-you Box — it arrives ready; you plug it in and connect your phone. The Membership and Kit tiers involve a one-time setup with a screen, for the DIY-inclined.

Why a lead time on the Box?

We run lean and build to order — no warehouse of aging hardware. Your box is sourced, imaged, tested, and shipped just for you. ~1–2 weeks.

Is this medical advice?

No. End of Days is an offline reference tool. It surfaces information from published manuals and clearly flags anything unverified. It is not a substitute for a doctor, and nothing here is professional medical, legal, or safety advice. Always use your own judgment and seek a professional when you can.
